Classic 350 Overview :


  • The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a retro-styled motorcycle that is one of the best-selling models from the brand.

  • The engine is mated to a five-speed gearbox.

  • The Classic 350 is built on a single downtube frame and features telescopic forks at the front and twin shock absorbers at the rear.

  • The motorcycle comes with a 13-litre fuel tank and weighs 195kg.

Engine & Performance :


  • Engine Type: 349cc, single-cylinder, air-oil cooled, SOHC

  • Max Power: 20.2 BHP @ 6100 RPM

  • Max Torque: 27 Nm @ 4000 RPM

  • Fuel System: Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI)

  • Top Speed: ~110 km/h

  • Mileage: Approx. 35-40 km/l

    • The engine is derived from the J-series platform, ensuring smooth power delivery, less vibration, and refined performance.


Brakes, Suspension & Wheels


  • Braking System :

    1. Single-channel ABS (front disc, rear drum)

    2. Dual-channel ABS (front and rear disc brakes)

  • Front Suspension: 41mm telescopic forks, 130mm travel

  • Rear Suspension: Twin shock absorbers with 6-step adjustable preload

  • Wheels:

    1. Spoke wheels (Tube tires) in standard variants

    2. Alloy wheels (Tubeless tires) in Dark Series

Features & Technology :


  • Semi-digital instrument cluster (Analog speedometer with LCD display for trip meter, fuel gauge, clock)

  • Tripper Navigation (Bluetooth-enabled turn-by-turn navigation in select variants)

  • USB Charging Port (For mobile charging on the go)

  • New Frame: Stronger double-cradle chassis for better stability

  • Self Start

Ride Comfort & Ergonomics


  • Wide handlebars for better control

  • Forward-set footpegs for relaxed riding posture

  • Soft cushioned seat for long-distance comfort

  • Low Seat Height: 805mm, suitable for riders of different heights

  • Weight: 195 kg (Heavier but provides a stable ride)
