Royal Enfield: A Legacy of Timeless Motorcycling

Introduction :

Royal Enfield is one of the world's oldest and most iconic motorcycle brands, known for its rugged design, powerful engines, and a rich heritage dating back to 1901. The brand, originally a British company, is now owned by Eicher Motors Limited (India) and has become a symbol of classic motorcycling.

History & Evolution :

1. 1901: The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in England.

2. 1955: The brand entered India and partnered with Madras Motors to manufacture motorcycles.

3. 1994: Eicher Motors acquired Royal Enfield, reviving and modernizing the brand.

4. Present: Royal Enfield has a global presence, producing motorcycles that blend retro aesthetics with modern engineering.
